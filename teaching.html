Teaching

My teaching introduces students to anthropology as a way of questioning familiar assumptions and connecting everyday experience to larger structures of power, history, and inequality.

Pedagogical Approach

I use ethnographic exercises, collaborative discussion, and scaffolded writing assignments to help students develop analytical confidence. My courses emphasize positionality, ethical research, careful observation, and the relationship between local worlds and global processes.
